Get to know me!

Hello! My name is Kyi Phyu Shyun Latt, and I'm a proud 22-year-old Burmese native, born and raised in the beautiful country of Myanmar. My journey in the world of Information Technology began back in 2019, igniting a passion that has only grown stronger with time. With a dream to become a brilliant data analyst and eventually a data scientist, I’ve embarked on an exciting and ever-evolving path.

My adventure into the tech realm started with learning HTML and Java, which later led me to explore graphic design. This creative aspect of IT piqued my interest and opened the door to endless possibilities within the digital world. However, while I enjoyed programming, I discovered an even greater passion for data handling and analysis. This revelation in 2024 marked the beginning of my focused journey towards becoming a data analyst.

I dived headfirst into learning the essential tools and languages for data analysis, starting with SQL and MySQL, and progressing to Excel, Power BI, Tableau, Python, and Pandas. Although I am still honing my skills, I have reached a level of understanding that allows me to undertake projects independently. I’ve learned the critical steps of data cleaning, visualization, exploration, storage, and analysis, each revealing unique stories hidden within the data. It’s a fascinating process that I’ve grown to love, and I am committed to uncovering these stories in greater detail.

Currently, I am an ICT student at Rangsit University in Pathum Thani, Bangkok. My studies have expanded my knowledge base, allowing me to delve into programming languages like Python and Java, as well as frontend development using HTML and CSS. This diverse skill set enables me to create my own websites and further enrich my IT expertise.

Throughout this journey, I have found immense joy in learning and exploring new technologies. The thrill of acquiring new skills and refining the ones I already possess keeps me motivated and driven. My quest for knowledge is never-ending, and I am dedicated to becoming a professional data analyst, constantly striving to improve and excel in my field.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I take immense pride in my Burmese heritage. It shapes my perspective and fuels my ambition to make a meaningful impact in the world of data analysis. Every dataset has a story to tell, and I am here to uncover and narrate those stories with precision and clarity.

As I continue on this exhilarating journey, I look forward to embracing new challenges, expanding my horizons, and ultimately achieving my goal of becoming a top-tier data analyst and data scientist. The world of technology is vast and ever-changing, and I am excited to be a part of it, learning, growing, and making my mark in the fascinating field of data analysis.
